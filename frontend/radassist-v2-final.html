How a report is produced
Seven stages. The last one is a person.
01
Chest X-ray
Uploaded by the reporting doctor
02
PHI protection
Identifiers masked before embedding
03
BiomedCLIP
Encoded to a 512-d vector
04
ChromaDB
Nearest prior cases retrieved
05
Similar cases
Findings aggregated into evidence
06
AI draft
Llama 3 writes only from that evidence
07
Radiologist review
The doctor edits, signs, and owns the report. Nothing is final without this step.
